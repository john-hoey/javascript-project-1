• Memory Game

HOW TO PLAY:

Click "Start Game" to start the timer and begin the game. Click two cards in the grid to turn them face up. If those cards match, they are removed from the grid and the player continues by selecting two more cards. Once all the cards are matched and removed, the game ends and the timer stops. Try to complete the game in the shortest amount of time possible. If you want to start over, click the reset button.
